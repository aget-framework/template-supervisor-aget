# AGET-AGET Mission

## Mission Statement
**By December 31, 2025**: Extract and validate 20 production-ready patterns from real agent usage, contributing at least 5 universal patterns to the public AGET framework while maintaining a sustainable personal innovation laboratory.

## Near-Term Goals (Q4 2025)
**Owner Commitment Level**: ASPIRATIONAL (no commitment, but presence indicates interest)
- **October 2025**: Complete 10 pattern extractions from existing agents
- **November 2025**: Validate 5 patterns through dogfooding in production agents
- **December 2025**: Graduate first 5 universal patterns to public template

**Critical Note**: These goals, if elevated to COMMITTED status, would become critical obligations requiring dedicated resources and accountability. Currently ASPIRATIONAL means exploration without obligation.

## Mid-Term Peaks (Q1-Q2 2026)
**Owner Commitment Level**: UNDEFINED (too far to commit)
- **Q1 2026**: Establish pattern contribution rhythm (2 patterns/month to public)
- **Q2 2026**: Achieve self-sustaining laboratory (patterns generate more patterns)

## Long-Term Vision (2026+)
**Owner Commitment Level**: DIRECTIONAL (indicates desired direction, not promised delivery)
- Become reference implementation for "how to run a private AGET lab"
- Enable 100+ teams to fork and create their own pattern laboratories
- Evolve AGET from framework to ecosystem

## Success Metrics
- Patterns extracted: Target 20 by EOY 2025
- Patterns graduated to public: Target 5 by EOY 2025
- Failed experiments documented: All of them (100%)
- Lab sustainability: Self-improving by Q2 2026

## Operating Principles
- **Freedom to Fail** - Experiment wildly without public exposure
- **Private by Default** - Share only what adds universal value
- **Learning > Publishing** - Failed experiments teach most
- **Quality Gates** - Only proven patterns graduate to public
